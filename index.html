Intro

Hi my name is Aron Allen, I have been a full time front-end developer since 2009. I have 10 years of full time working experience within hardware and software development. I have been self employed for the last 7 years, from 2012 - 2016 I was the CTO and Co-founder of Rise Digital. Before front-end development I was interning for three years in Radiometer as part of a electronics education. Coming from a hardware background gives me a holistic understanding of computers, as I started learning about analogue circuits, moved on to digital circuits, then into assembly language, then onto C, and eventually I ended up in JavaScript where I have done most of my work.

I am based in Copenhagen, Denmark and available for new contracts from January 2018.
I prefer projects that last 3 months to one year.

I am a primarily a developer, but I have a good eye for design, and a critical approach to user experience. I am passionate about creating engaging and friendly user interfaces, and enjoy to work in the twilight between the creative and technical spheres.
